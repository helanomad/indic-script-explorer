Tamil-Brahmi is a southern regional tradition of the Brahmi script, adapted to write Old Tamil in early inscriptions.

This interactive Tamil-Brahmi Alphabet chart is part of the Indic Script Explorer by Hela Nomad. It presents the core letters and signs of the Tamil-Brahmi script tradition, with Romanization and pronunciation.

Script Notes: Historical script, modern font

What you see here is a standardized digital representation of Tamil-Brahmi.
Tamil-Brahmi varies across regions and periods, so inscriptional forms can
look quite different.

These forms are a standardized scholarly reconstruction and are not tied to a
single century or region.
